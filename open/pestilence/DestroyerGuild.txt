THEME:
   The destroyers are a mix of a warrior, and an incredible hulk type.
They utilize there enhanced physical strength in combat rather than
magic spells and trickery.

Advantages;
   A few so called spells- which are made more as if there are skills.
A wc bonus of 1 for each of the 10 guild levels.  Str bonus up to 5 additional

Disadvantages:
   Unable to wear boots,underwear armor.  Due to physical strengths
it takes away from mental awareness, so mag is limited to 15 and 
intelligence to 10.  Level 10 to join with no free xps to be brought
into the guild.  No game spells.

/players/pestilence/closed/destroyer/powers and /cmds

Skills 

blindside
 A skill only to be used to start a fight, cannot be used if a mob is under 10/10 or your
already in combat with it.  No other skill can be used to start a fight
    Damage random 35 cost 20 sps

barrage
 An attack that appears to be 1 additional weapon attack, but does spell damage not actual
physical damage.   damage 10+random 20    cost 25sps 

rush
 A physical attack in a charging fashion.
   Damage random(strength*3/2 ) max of 20 cost 9 sps

disable
 blow to the knee preventing a mob from wimpying
   chance of miss if random100> wil+pie+random 100
   miss cost 20 sps  hit cost 35

weaken
 lower ac by 4 and raise wc by 2 only once on a mob
 cost 75 sps

death  
 A fatal deathblow cost 1/4 of mob's remaing hps

dlead
 Take lead in a fight 10 sps
  
decorpse
 drain corpse of it's dna, and heals yourself at value of corpse

frenzy
 An attack that appears to have between 2 and 3 extra weapon attacks
must have weapon wielded for this attack.  No actual damage is done from weapon
just the appearance it does.
  Damage 15+random 30   cost 50 sps

rage
 raging blow where you charge the opponent
  Damage 10+random 30  cost 30 hps 15 sps
