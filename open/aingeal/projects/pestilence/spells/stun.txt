~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
                 S T U N
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Usage:	stun <NPC name>
	
Description: 	This spell temporarily
		weakens and slows your 
		opponent, thus 	rendering 
		your opponent unable to 
		leave the room as his end 
		grows near. This spell 
		may not be used against 
		another player.
	
Minimum Guild Level: 7
