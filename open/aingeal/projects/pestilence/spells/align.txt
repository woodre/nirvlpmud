~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
              A L I G N
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Usage:	align
	align <	NPC name>
	align <player name>

Description: 	You can determine the alignment
		of NPCs or other players with 
		this command.  If you do not
		enter an NPC or player name,
		you will automatically determine
		your own alignment.
	
Minimum Guild Level: 1
