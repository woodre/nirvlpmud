Usage:	leave_guild or guild_leave

Description: Allows you to leave the guild.
             If you leave the Dark Order,
	     you purchase your freedom at
	     the cost of all the experience
	     you have paid into the Dark
	     Order, plus half of your total
	     experience.
	
Minimum Guild Level: 1
