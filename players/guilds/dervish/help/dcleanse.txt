
DERVISH ABILITY - DCLEANSE

Cost:  Unknown
Level: 19
Delay: Medium

Function:

This Master ability allows the Dervish to detoxify using the cleansing
power of the Sands.  Its power increases as you achieve higher extra
levels.  It requires the firmest of devotion in order to perform.
Legend speaks that the bond between Sands and Dervish becomes
more tenuous as usage of this spell persists.
