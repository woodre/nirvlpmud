>> ORDER: EXPLODE SELF AT LOCATION

Usage:
order <indexnum> explodespot <roomfile>

Example:
'order 3 explodespot room/wild1'

Description:
This command turns the monster into a suicide bomb detonating at the
location specified. Unlike the similar command 'explode' the monster
does not do damage to the players, but might leave a sticky residue
all over them.
