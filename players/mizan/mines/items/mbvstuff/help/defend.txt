>> ORDER: DEFEND POSITION

Usage:
order <indexnum> defend <roomfile> <duration>

Example:
'order 3 defend room/wild1 10'

Description:
This command issued to a monster makes it aggressively defend a room.
Any player, monster, pet, mudkid, anything that enters the room that
is not a monster guild member will be attacked.

Be careful when using this command especially in an area where newbies
would frequent. The monster will hold this position and fight as long
as it can.
