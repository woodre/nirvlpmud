C Y B E R N I N J A

Ability: plant_mine
Usage  : plant_mine

Effect : Places a landmine in your current room with a
         short delay. After the delay time has elapsed,
         the mine will attempt to explode on and damage
         the first PK, non Cyberninja player who enters
         the room.
        
         Only one landmine may be placed in each room at
         a time.
         
Must be PK to use.
