Economy Daemon

Values
	e_cap:		Maximum total value possible of funds in circulation
	e_cur:		Current total value of funds in circulation
	e_avail:	Amount of funds available for distribution (e_cap-e_cur)
	p_cur:		Total value of current player holdings
	s_loan_num:	Number of shop loans outstanding
	s_loan_val:	Value of outstanding shop loans
	s_ids[]:	Array of id's of shops with oustanding loans
	s_loan_bal[]:	Array of balances owed by shops indexed by s_ids
