SmartWeapon Links
Usage: target <opponent>

    SmartWeapon Links allow faster mind-to-weapon control. The ninja
utilizing these tech devices will be much more combat-effective when
wielding a specially-designed CyberNinja weapon. All of the weapons
in the CyberNinja weaponry are SmartWeapons. With each subsequent
link, the weapon control becomes faster and more effective.
Smartweapon links allow CyberNinjas to target specific opponents by 
channeling energy into target-lock technology.
