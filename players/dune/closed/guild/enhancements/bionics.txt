Bionics
Usage: bionics (bion)

     Bionics are powerful mechanical parts which enhance/replace
certain organs/tissues of your body.  Use of bionics is only in
combat, so activate them in combat.  Activation requires a small
amount of energy.  Once activated though, your damage will be
enhanced an amount depending on your quality level.  Bionics
automatically disengage when the combat is over.
Bionics and blades may be used together.
