_____________KEY TO NETROPOLIS_______________________________
archives      read guild history
chopshop      purchase enhancements, join/leave guild
conversion    convert hit points <-> spell points, healing
council       meeting place for specific guild discussions
emoteon       create/view custom guild emotes
entrance      entrance to guild, allows fixing of guild object
fabrication   upgrade neural processors, exchange guild xp
guildhall     main social area, guild bulletin board
mechshop      place to buy mechs
shop          shop of ninja items
teleport      many guild exits all over Nirvana
weaponry      select/change ninja weapons, smartweapon links
