         Corpse Parts

     You can harvest the following parts for use:

     blood - Draining the bodily fluids from a freshly killed victim
             is the most frequently used and harvested part
     skin  - Stripping the flesh from a corpse with the harvesting
             tool for a basic component
     eye   - Just two of these valuable components are available per
             corpse 
     spine - An intact spine can be of value if taken from a powerful
             enough corpse.  A rare component
     heart - A very valuable component when taken from a powerful
             enough corpse. A rare component
     soul  - Harvesting a soul involves trapping it within a small
             fragile crystal. Very rare component

     All components are stored within the component pouch
