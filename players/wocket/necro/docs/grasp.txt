         Grasp (offensive)

     With proper dedication and desire, a necromancer may learn
     to limit the ability of an opponent to use magic, or at the
     very least, rob the opponent of their depth of magic power. 

     Guild Level Attained: 2
     Spell Point Cost:  15
     Material Components:  2 parts blood

     Usage: grasp <target>
