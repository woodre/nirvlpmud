              NECROMANCERS
---------------------------------------------------------
     Preserve Corpse (miscellaneous)

Once a corpse is attained, a Necromancer can perform a
ritual that will perfectly preserve any corpse for later
use. Many Necromancers find that corpses make great
room decorations.
Basic Necromancer Ability.

     Guild Level Attained: 1
     Spell Point Cost: 5
     Material Components: None
     Usage: preserve
