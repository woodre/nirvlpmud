              NECROMANCERS
---------------------------------------------------------
     Harvesting Parts (miscellaneous)

Once the corpse of a victim is properly attained, a 
Necromancer can then harvest body parts from the corpse
to further increase their use of magic. Almost every spell
that a necromancer can cast needs a material component
of some sort taken and then sacrificed to the realm of 
death during spell casting. Basic Necromancer Ability.

NOTICE- Parts cannot be harvested unless a necromancer 
        wielding a harvesting tool

     Guild Level Attained: 1
     Spell Point Cost: None
     Material Components: None
     Usage: harvest <type> #
            type = What type of part to harvest
            # = how many of the part you wish to harvest
