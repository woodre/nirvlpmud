              NECROMANCERS
---------------------------------------------------------
     Death's Doorway (miscellaneous)

A portal, or doorway, made from the purest bone rises from
the ground at the casting of this spell, allowing the
necromancer to return to the crypt below the guild hall. 
Basic Necromancer Ability.

     Guild Level Attained: 2
     Spell Point Cost: 40
     Material Components: 3 parts Blood
     Usage: ddoor
