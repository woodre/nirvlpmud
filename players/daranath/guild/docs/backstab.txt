              NECROMANCERS
---------------------------------------------------------
     Backstab (offensive)

Assassinations occur on a regular basis within the realm of
death. This ability has been passed on from the spirits to
the guild. You can sneak up on a foe and try to assassinate
them, fully ending thier existance in one swift stroke.
Basic Necromancer Ability.

NOTICE- You must do this before combat has started and you
        must have the harvesting tool wielded.

     Guild Level Attained: 2
     Spell Point Cost: 25 plus 10 per guild level
     Material Components: None
     Usage: backstab <target>
