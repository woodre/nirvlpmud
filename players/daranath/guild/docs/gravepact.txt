              NECROMANCERS
---------------------------------------------------------
     Gravepact Spell (misc)

The Gravepact is one of the most powerful and dangerous spells
a Necromancer can wield. The gravepact causes a rift to open
to the Realm of Death and then the denziens of the realms are
allowed to determine the course the magical energy shall take.
Always a dangerous course of action...

NOTICE - Both beneficial and detremental effects can happen
         from the use of this spell. management not responsible

     Guild Level Attained: 1
     Spell Point Cost: 25
     Material Components: 3 parts Blood
     Usage: gravepact <target>
