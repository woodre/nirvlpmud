              NECROMANCERS
---------------------------------------------------------
     Death's Embrace (miscellaneous)

The embrace of death is the closest any member of the 
Necromancers can actually come to joining the realm of
death. This action causes the necromancer to sacrifice
themself to the magic, taking vital spell component from
their own body. Basic Necromancer Ability.

NOTICE- YOU WILL DIE WHEN YOU USE THIS ABILITY

     Guild Level Attained: 1
     Spell Point Cost: 25
     Material Components: 1 point of Stamina
              You must be at least level 2 to use this
     Usage: embrace_death
