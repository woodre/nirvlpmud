              NECROMANCERS
---------------------------------------------------------
     Sadistic Glee (offensive)

Sadistic Glee continues the trend of other Necromancer spells
such as Sphere of Anguish and Spectral Grasp. This can be
used to defend a Necromancer against opponents that other
spells will not work upon.
Advanced Necromancer Spell.

Note -> This spell can be used against other players
Note2 -> The Realm of Death is not always pleased when a 
         Necromancer abandons its spells for others.

     Guild Level Attained: 6
     Spell Point Cost: 20
     Material Components: 1 part Skin
                          3 parts Blood
     Usage: glee <target>
