                    -- DOUBLE STRIKE --

DEX REQ: 10
XP COST: 1,000,000
LEVEL REQ: 19
COIN COST: 30,000
WEAPON REQ: A weapon skill at 5
SKILL TYPE: Automatic

This is a very powerful skill that allows the wielders weapon to double strike
in a round.  Some people say this is just a slight of hand, then they get hit
by it.
