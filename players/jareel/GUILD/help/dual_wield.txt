                    -- Dual Wield --

DEX REQ: 20
XP COST: 1,000,000
LEVEL REQ: 19
COIN COST: 50,000
WEAPON REQ: A weapon skill at 5
DRAWBACK: Can not use a shield when off-wielding
SKILL TYPE: Activated

This is a very powerful skill it allows the possessor of this skill to 
wield two weapons.  Some people call this the Whirlwind.
