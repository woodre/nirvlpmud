                    -- BLADESHARP --

TYPE: Spell (Variable)
INT REQ: 1/level
USAGE: bladesharp (Must have a wielded weapon)
COST TO MAX: 100,000 xp

Bladesharp can only be cast upon a bladed weapon. Make the weapon
smack up your foe.  Effect only lasts a limited time.
