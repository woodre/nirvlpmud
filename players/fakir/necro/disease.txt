           Disease (offensive)

     As a necromancer gains knowlege and experience from the realm
     of death, he learns to controll the very essence of certain
     types of disease.  Using this knowlege, he developes the
     ability to cast a spell which, over a short period of time,
     causes his opponent to sicken and become weaker by the moment.
        

     Guild Level Attained: 5
     Spell Point Cost: 45sp 
     Material Components: 1 heart
                          4 skins
                         
     Usage: disease (adversary name)
