              ~~~~ Obillusion ~~~~

   Command: obillusion <desc>
   Cost:    20

     Will create an illusion of an object. For example if you
     did 'obillusion A large sword' it would form an illusion 
     of A large sword in the room. To get rid of it you can
     kick, punch, or kill illusion. See 'color_help' for more
     info.
