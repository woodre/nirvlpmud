              ~~~~ Drakyr ~~~~

   Command: drakyr
   Cost:    150

     You may summon an enchanted drakyr, a man-creature from
     the lost world of the Deimos.  While riding this foul
     beast, you may level a heavier attack upon your hated
     prey.

     The Drakyr has the following commands.:
       mount - to mount the Drakyr.
       dismount - to dismount the Drakyr.
       release - to get rid of the Drakyr.
       dbag - to store items in Drakyr's bag.
         dbag with no argument will list current bag contents.
       dunbag - to retrieve items from Drakyr's bag.
