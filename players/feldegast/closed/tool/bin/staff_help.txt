Wizard Staff Help
------------------
Wlog <str>             : log an action
cg <str>               : color gossip
clean                  : clean a room of all nonplayer objects
familiar               : summon a familiar
filei                  : list player inventory (files only)
debunk                 : remove all mythos guild objects
noidle                 : Turn off idle quit
append                : Append <file1> to <file2>
flist                 : Show all of <arg1>'s objects on players.
detain                : Move a player to the tower's prison.
